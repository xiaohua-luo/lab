# Xiao-Hua Luo's Lab



We study virus-driven immune remodeling after HSCT and CAR-T and develop immunotherapies in AML to improve immune recovery and disease control. By integrating HSCT, CAR-T, and other immune-based strategies, we aim to advance precision treatment for hematologic malignancies.
